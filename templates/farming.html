How it works

1Start with at least 1,000 G$. Every 100 G$ buys 1 chicken, so the minimum farm starts with 10 chickens.
2Each chicken produces 1 egg per day for 30 days. The egg value streams up to 100 G$ profit for a 1,000 G$ farm.
3Sell eggs during the cycle, or wait until maturity. After 30 days, closing the farm returns principal plus remaining egg profit.
4Minimum final withdrawal is hardcoded at {{ farming_config.min_withdraw_gd }} G$ in the smart contract.
