Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes what information GoodMarket (goodmarket.live, the "Service") collects when you use it, how we use that information, and the choices you have. GoodMarket is operated by the GoodMarket project author ("we", "us", "our"). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

GoodMarket is designed to minimize the personal data it handles. Because the Service is wallet-based, your primary identifier is the Celo wallet address you connect with. Specifically, we collect:

Data Purpose Retention
Wallet address (EVM / Celo) Identify your account, track reward eligibility, gate access to features. Stored while the account is active.
Username (optional, user-chosen) Community leaderboards, public profile. Stored while the account is active; you can change or clear it.
Task completion records (quiz scores, game results, social-task submissions, referral codes used) Compute earned rewards, prevent duplicate claims, fraud detection. Retained indefinitely for audit and anti-abuse purposes.
On-chain transaction hashes Display earning history, verify UBI claims, reconcile rewards with blockchain state. Retained indefinitely; also permanently public on the Celo blockchain.
Optional linked identifiers you choose to connect (Telegram ID, Twitter/X handle, Discourse handle) Verify that you completed the corresponding social task. Stored while you keep the integration connected.
Session cookie Keep you logged in between page loads. Session-lifetime; cleared on logout or session expiry.
Server logs (IP address, timestamp, request path, user-agent) Security, abuse detection, debugging. Rotated; typically ≤ 30 days.

We do not collect or ask for: passwords, seed phrases, private keys, government IDs, real-name information, home addresses, phone numbers (outside of optional integrations you initiate), or payment card data. GoodMarket is non-custodial — your wallet's private key never leaves your wallet provider.

2. How we use your information

  • Operate the core features (UBI, savings, swap, learn-and-earn, tasks, referrals).
  • Calculate and disburse rewards, prevent double-spending, and enforce daily / cooldown limits.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, sybil attacks, and bot farming.
  • Communicate important updates (e.g. via Telegram channel announcements or in-app banners).
  • Improve the Service using aggregated / anonymized analytics.

3. Third-party services we use

Your data may flow through these third parties as necessary for the Service to function. They process data under their own terms and privacy policies:

  • Supabase (PostgreSQL) — database hosting for account records, task completions, rewards.
  • Vercel — web hosting and CDN.
  • Celo / Forno — blockchain RPC endpoints (forno.celo.org), Celoscan explorer.
  • WalletConnect — wallet-connection relay used when you link an external wallet.
  • Opera MiniPay — when you use GoodMarket inside the MiniPay dApp browser.
  • Telegram — Mini App SDK, bot integration for task verification and notifications.
  • Twitter/X — embeds and API calls for verifying Twitter social tasks.
  • Discourse (GoodDollar forum) — verifying Discourse task participation.
  • ImgBB — image uploads (profile pictures, community stories).
  • Reloadly — gift-card / top-up API (only if you use that feature).
  • Turnkey — embedded-wallet infrastructure for users who create a new wallet in-app.
  • GoodDollar protocol — identity verification and UBI claim mechanics.
  • Uniswap (on Celo) — on-chain swap routing.

4. Cookies and local storage

GoodMarket uses a Flask session cookie to maintain login state. We may also use localStorage / sessionStorage to cache your wallet address, preferences, and onboarding progress so you don't have to reconnect on every visit. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

5. Public information

Some information is inherently public: on-chain transactions, wallet addresses, token balances, leaderboard rankings, community story posts, and any profile data you publish. Do not share on GoodMarket anything you do not want publicly visible.

6. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account records (HTTPS everywhere, encrypted database connections, access controls on admin endpoints). However, no system is perfectly secure, and you are responsible for securing your wallet, recovery phrase, and devices.

7. Your rights

  • Access / export: You may request a copy of the account data associated with your wallet.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of account metadata (username, linked handles, task records). Note that on-chain transaction history cannot be deleted because it lives on the public Celo blockchain.
  • Correction: You may update optional profile fields yourself from within the app.
  • Withdraw consent: You may disconnect your wallet or stop using the Service at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email chadtreb1999@gmail.com from an address you can verify, and reference the wallet address involved.

8. Children

GoodMarket is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has connected a wallet, contact us and we will remove the associated records.

9. International transfers

Our infrastructure and third-party providers may process data in jurisdictions other than yours (including the United States and the European Union). By using the Service you consent to such transfers.
